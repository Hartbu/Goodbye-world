Goodbye World

A program to greet the planet.

This is an attempt to greet everyone on Earth using our various human languages. we're interested to see how long our list can become

we'll start with languages that the Acedu staff could offer without using Google.

  • English: Goodbye, world
  • yoruba: o dabo aye
  • Japanese: sayonra sekia
  • French: au revoir le monde
  • igbo: daalu uwa
  • spanish: adios mundo
